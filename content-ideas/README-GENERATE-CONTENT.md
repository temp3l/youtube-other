From my perspective:

## Lowest-Copyright-Risk Content Strategy

### Do

- Watch existing videos for topic discovery.
- Extract facts, timelines, concepts, and research.

### Don't

- Reuse transcripts.
- Reuse footage, audio, thumbnails, or images.

### Safest Formula

1. Find a popular video.
2. Research the underlying facts.
3. Write a completely new script.
4. Generate your own voice-over.
5. Generate your own visuals.

### Recommended Starting Order

1. History & Historical Mysteries
2. Psychology & Human Behavior
3. Science & Space
4. Mythology & Folklore
5. Programming & Technology

**Copyright risk for all five:** Low, provided you recreate the content from facts and research rather than copying the original video's wording, visuals, audio, or structure.
