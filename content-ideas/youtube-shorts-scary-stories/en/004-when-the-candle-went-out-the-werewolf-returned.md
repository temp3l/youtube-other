# When the Candle Went Out, the Werewolf Returned

In Morbach, Germany, a small candle has supposedly burned for centuries.

According to legend, it keeps a werewolf inside its grave.

In 1988, American soldiers passed the roadside shrine.

For the first time, the candle was out.

Minutes later, an alarm sounded at their military installation.

A figure almost two meters tall stood at the fence.

Half man.

Half wolf.

The guard dogs whimpered and refused to approach.

Before the soldiers could fire, the creature vanished.

Then it appeared again.

This time, it was inside the secured compound.

It charged directly at them.

Just before it reached the soldiers, a woman's scream came from the forest.

The creature stopped and disappeared.

The following morning, the candle was lit again.

People in Morbach still make sure it never goes out.
