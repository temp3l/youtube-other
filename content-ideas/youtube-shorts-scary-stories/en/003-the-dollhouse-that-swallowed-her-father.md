# The Dollhouse That Swallowed Her Father

A man began sleepwalking every night.

His wife tied a small bell around his wrist so she would hear him move.

But every night, his path led toward his daughter's dollhouse.

Then a new figure appeared inside it.

The doll looked exactly like the father.

During the next night, the bell rang faster and faster.

The man stood before the dollhouse and whispered:

“It is calling me.”

His wife grabbed him.

The bell suddenly stopped.

The man disappeared.

No open door.

No window.

No footprints.

When the mother told her daughter that her father was gone, the girl laughed.

“Daddy said he would never leave me.”

She pointed at the dollhouse.

Inside the tiny basement lay a new doll.

It wore her father's pajamas.

And around its miniature wrist hung the bell.
