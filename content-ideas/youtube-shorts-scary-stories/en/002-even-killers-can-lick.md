# Even Killers Can Lick

A woman lived alone with her dog.

Every night, she let one hand hang over the edge of the bed.

When the dog licked her fingers, she knew she was safe.

One night, she woke to a dripping sound.

**Drip. Drip. Drip.**

She checked the faucet and returned to bed.

Her hand fell over the edge.

Something licked it.

Later, the dripping returned.

This time, it came from the bathroom.

She pulled back the shower curtain.

Her dog was hanging from the shower fixture.

Blood dripped from its fur.

A note was attached to its collar:

**“Even killers can lick.”**

Then she heard movement behind her.

Someone was crawling out from beneath her bed.

The man had been there all night.

And every time she lowered her hand, it had never been her dog licking her.
