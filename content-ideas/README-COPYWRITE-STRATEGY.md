## 1. History & Historical Mysteries

### Pros

- Facts are not copyrighted.
- Huge amount of existing content to research and re-explain.

### Cons

- Requires fact-checking.
- Many channels already compete in this niche.

---

## 2. Psychology & Human Behavior

### Pros

- Strong retention and curiosity.
- Easy to create original scripts from research papers and studies.

### Cons

- Risk of oversimplifying science.
- Requires careful sourcing.

---

## 3. Science & Space

### Pros

- Evergreen content.
- Facts and scientific concepts are not copyrighted.

### Cons

- Requires accuracy.
- Visuals can be more expensive to produce well.

---

## 4. Programming & Technology

### Pros

- High CPM and monetization potential.
- Easy to create original educational content.

### Cons

- Smaller audience than general-interest niches.
- Content can become outdated quickly.

---

## 5. Mythology & Folklore

### Pros

- Most source material is public domain.
- Excellent fit for AI-generated visuals and storytelling.

### Cons

- Requires engaging narration to stand out.
- Some stories are heavily covered already.

---

## Lowest-Copyright-Risk Content Strategy

### Do

- Watch existing videos for topic discovery.
- Extract facts, timelines, concepts, and research.

### Don't

- Reuse transcripts.
- Reuse footage, audio, thumbnails, or images.

### Safest Formula

1. Find a popular video.
2. Research the underlying facts.
3. Write a completely new script.
4. Generate your own voice-over.
5. Generate your own visuals.

### Recommended Starting Order

1. History & Historical Mysteries
2. Psychology & Human Behavior
3. Science & Space
4. Mythology & Folklore
5. Programming & Technology

**Copyright risk for all five:** Low, provided you recreate the content from facts and research rather than copying the original video's wording, visuals, audio, or structure.
