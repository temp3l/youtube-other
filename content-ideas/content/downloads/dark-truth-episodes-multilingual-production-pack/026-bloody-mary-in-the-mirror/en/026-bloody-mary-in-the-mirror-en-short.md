## Narration Script

Mia said the name three times as a joke. Her reflection opened its eyes before she did. The
group enters an old washroom where students once performed the ritual. Each mirror begins
showing a different memory from her school years. The reflected figure claims it does not kill
people; it replaces the version they are ashamed of. The missing friend returns when Mia refuses
to deny what happened in the past. At home, Mia covers every mirror—except one that now reflects
the abandoned school.
