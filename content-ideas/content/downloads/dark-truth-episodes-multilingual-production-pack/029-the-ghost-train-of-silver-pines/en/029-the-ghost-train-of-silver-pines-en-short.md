## Narration Script

Silver Pines station closed forty years ago. At 4:19 every morning, a train still arrives. Lukas
visits after receiving an old timetable with one unlisted service. A black train arrives
silently and opens doors onto crowded carriages. The conductor asks Lukas for a ticket stamped
with the date of his death. He returns home, but railway clocks stop whenever he enters a
station. Years later, the same timetable arrives with his name printed beneath the 4:19 service.
