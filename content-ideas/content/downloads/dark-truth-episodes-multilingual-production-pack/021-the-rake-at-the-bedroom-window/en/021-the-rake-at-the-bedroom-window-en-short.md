## Narration Script

Paul's bedroom was on the second floor. There was no balcony, no tree and no ladder. Yet every
night, something tapped on the glass. Paul moves into the farmhouse after his brother claims a
pale figure watches him sleep. At 2:11 a.m., a hairless figure crouches beyond the glass and
copies Paul's breathing. Paul discovers muddy handprints on the ceiling above the bed, proving
it has already entered the room. Paul burns the photographs and the tapping stops. Months later,
a new recording shows Paul asleep while the camera slowly moves closer from beneath the bed.
