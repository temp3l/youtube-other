## Narration Script

The portrait showed a woman facing a window. After Clara cleaned it, the woman had turned to
face the room. Clara receives the unsigned portrait from an estate with instructions never to
expose the lower layer. Each morning, the figure stands closer to the foreground. A hidden
inscription says the portrait does not contain the woman; it contains everyone who has looked at
her. Clara seals the canvas beneath white primer and stores it in darkness. Months later, a
museum scan shows the woman standing beneath the fresh white layer with her hand pressed
outward.
