## Narration Script

The hotel had no fourteenth floor, but Sophie's keycard was printed with Room 1413. Staff insist
the card is a printing error and assign Sophie another room. Room 1413 contains luggage
belonging to guests reported missing across decades. Each time she refuses, the room changes to
a different year. The receptionist guides her to a service door that appears only while the call
remains connected. Sophie escapes, but her final hotel review is dated fourteen years before she
was born.
