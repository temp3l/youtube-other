## Narration Script

Mia was told never to clean a room marked with a black ribbon. At 3:10 a.m., the ribbon
appeared on her own apartment door. Mia takes a night job where an elderly cleaner named Mara
handles rooms no one else may enter. Mara claims she does not cause the deaths; she cleans away
what remains after death has already chosen. The room contains personal objects from future
deaths, including Mia's apartment key. Mara sacrifices her name to close the threshold,
becoming invisible to every living guest. Mia becomes the new cleaner, and her first assignment
is a room registered to someone watching the video.
