The immediate danger seemed to end there. The accident does not happen, but Room Six disappears from the corridor. The official explanation covered practical facts but not the impossible detail.
One final detail was discovered later. Tom later sees the missing room reflected in every dark window. That final piece of evidence changed the meaning of the apparent escape.
