## Narration Script

Elena raised her hand. Her reflection lowered its own and pointed behind her. Elena first
notices the delay during a late-night video call. It writes reversed messages in condensation
while Elena sleeps. Elena covers every reflective surface, but dark computer screens continue
showing the other apartment. The reflection has the scar on the wrong side and smiles. After the
mirror shatters, Elena's friends notice she no longer appears in photographs.
