## Narration Script

A masked woman asked Paul whether she was beautiful. Every answer he gave changed the street
around him. Paul hears students discussing a woman who appears near closed stations and asks
strangers a question. She asks, 'Am I beautiful?' Paul replies politely, but the street repeats
and returns him to the same vending machine. Remembering the legend, Paul gives an ambiguous
answer and throws wrapped sweets into the road as a distraction. Paul answers, 'You are not
asking me,' breaking eye contact and addressing the empty street. The woman vanishes, but every
photograph of Paul taken afterward shows a masked figure waiting behind him.
