## Narration Script

Laura was warned never to whistle back in the forest. The first time she did, the answer came
from directly behind her. Laura begins a two-night survey in an area where several hikers have
vanished. After she copies the tune, her radio repeats the whistle on an unused frequency. An
abandoned camp contains maps covered with the same warning: FAR MEANS NEAR. Laura reaches the
road by walking toward the loudest whistle, knowing it must be farthest away. Back at
headquarters, she hears the tune through an office ventilation grille.
