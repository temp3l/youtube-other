## Narration Script

Mara's train passed her stop and continued for twenty minutes without slowing. Then it arrived
at Kisaragi Station—a place missing from every map. Mara notices the carriage is empty and the
train no longer stops at familiar stations. Online messages advise her to remain on the train,
but the doors close after she steps out. A one-legged man appears along the tracks, then
vanishes when a train horn sounds. A train arrives containing passengers who all look like Mara
at different ages. She boards and returns to her city, but every station sign now briefly
changes to Kisaragi when she looks away.
