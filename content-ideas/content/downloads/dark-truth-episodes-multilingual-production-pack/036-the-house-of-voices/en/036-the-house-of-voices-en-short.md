## Narration Script

The house did not echo what Nina said. It repeated her words several seconds before she said
them. Nina is hired to archive tapes left in the studio basement. Rooms begin playing voices of
people who died in the house. She discovers microphones hidden in walls but none are connected.
The booth opens and releases a warm current of air carrying hundreds of names. After leaving,
Nina receives a blank audio file that whispers the first sentence of her next conversation.
