## Narration Script

Sophie found a black rose on the counter each morning. Every person named on its tag died
before the flower opened. Sophie inherits a flower shop and discovers a sealed basement
greenhouse. The named customer dies in an accident before sunset, and the rose opens at the
same moment. Sophie tries to destroy them, but burned petals reappear inside her mouth while
she sleeps. Rather than choose another victim, Sophie locks herself in the greenhouse and cuts
every root. The roses die, but one black bud later appears in a bouquet delivered to a newborn
child.
