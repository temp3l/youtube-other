## Narration Script

Nine experienced hikers cut their tent open from the inside and fled into deadly cold without
proper clothing. The question is not whether they were frightened. It is what made the tent
feel more dangerous than the storm. The group establishes camp on the slope of Kholat Syakhl
during worsening weather. Tracks lead downhill toward trees, where the first bodies are
discovered near a small fire. Several bodies recovered later show severe internal injuries,
fuelling decades of speculation. The reconstruction shows how a chain of ordinary hazards could
become fatal without claiming certainty. The ending emphasises that mystery survives because
the final minutes were never recorded.
