## Narration Script

The old woman gave Nina one instruction: keep the candle burning until sunrise. When the flame
died, something outside stood on two legs. Nina becomes stranded near a remote village after
photographing unusually large wolf tracks. The widow explains that the flame does not repel the
creature; it reminds it which body belongs to it. Nina sees a man in the snow wearing the
missing ranger's coat, but his knees bend the wrong way. Nina relights the candle using the
fireplace and forces the two halves to merge before dawn. Months later, every photograph from
that night shows Nina casting a wolf-shaped shadow.
