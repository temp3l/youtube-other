## Narration Script

Felix picked up a young woman walking alone in the rain. Ten minutes later, she vanished from
the locked back seat. The woman asks to be taken to an isolated farmhouse several kilometres
away. Felix notices the seat beneath her becoming soaked even though her coat is dry. At the
cross, a second version of the woman stands in the road. He returns and blocks the road just
before a family car reaches black ice. The rescued child later tells police that a wet woman was
sitting beside Felix the entire time.
