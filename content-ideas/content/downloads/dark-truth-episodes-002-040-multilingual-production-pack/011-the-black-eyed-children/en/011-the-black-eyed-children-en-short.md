## Narration Script

Two children stood outside Noah's motel room in freezing rain. They did not want shelter. They
wanted permission. Noah checks into an almost empty motel after an ambulance transfer. The
motel manager warns Noah not to invite anyone inside after midnight. When one looks up, its
eyes are completely black and reflect no light. He revokes every invitation by stating his full
name and address, reclaiming the room as his. At dawn, the children are gone, but two wet
footprints begin inside the room and stop beside his bed.
