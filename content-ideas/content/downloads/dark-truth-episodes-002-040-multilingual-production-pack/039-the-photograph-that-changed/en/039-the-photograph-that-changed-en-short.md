## Narration Script

The old family photograph changed every night. First, a stranger appeared near the trees. Then
he stood behind Felix's mother. Felix restores a damaged photograph from a 1978 picnic. A
blurred man advances toward the family each time the print changes. Felix discovers the stranger
resembles a brother his grandmother claimed never existed. The new image restores the missing
relatives but places Felix among them as a child. His current identification records disappear
the following morning.
