## Narration Script

Elena believed the dog beneath her bed was calming her. By morning, the dog was dead in the
hallway—and someone had written a message on the mirror. Her aunt leaves for a medical
conference and asks Elena to care for Bramble, an old golden retriever who always sleeps beside
the bed. Each time she reaches below the bed, something licks her hand. She assumes Bramble is
frightened by thunder. On the steamed mirror, a finger has written: HUMANS CAN LICK TOO. Police
find a nest in the attic containing food wrappers, photographs of Elena asleep, and a notebook
recording every night she visited the house. The final page says: SHE REACHED DOWN FIRST.
