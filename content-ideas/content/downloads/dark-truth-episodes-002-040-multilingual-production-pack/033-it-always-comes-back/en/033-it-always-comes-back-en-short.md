## Narration Script

Daniel burned the box, buried the ashes and left town. The next morning, it was waiting on his
kitchen table. The box has no hinges and contains a faint knocking sound. Daniel throws it into
the lake, but it appears wet beneath his bed. Each return is accompanied by one missing object
from Daniel's life. He seals himself inside the cabin and burns both structures together.
Firefighters find no body, only the undamaged box sitting in the ashes.
