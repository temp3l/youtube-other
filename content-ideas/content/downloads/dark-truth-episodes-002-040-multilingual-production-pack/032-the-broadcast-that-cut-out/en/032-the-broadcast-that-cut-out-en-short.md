## Narration Script

The emergency broadcast cut in without authorisation. Instead of a weather warning, it displayed
Mara's name and the time 4:12 a.m. Mara traces the signal to an unused transmitter
decommissioned in the 1980s. A distorted presenter appears and calmly describes events before
they happen. Mara finds archived tapes of identical broadcasts from previous disasters. At 4:12,
the broadcast cuts to black and the storm stops instantly. The station later airs normally, but
one frame of every programme contains Mara sitting in the empty control room.
