## Narration Script

Laura filmed the doll without asking permission. That night, it appeared in the background of a
photograph taken inside her locked apartment. Laura visits a museum display and dismisses the
custom of asking the doll's permission before photographing it. In each new image, the doll
appears closer to her apartment door. She wakes to find the doll's sailor suit hanging from her
bedroom chair, although the museum confirms the exhibit is untouched. The doll's expression
appears unchanged, but her missing memory card rests inside the case. The final recovered
photograph shows Laura asleep at home and the museum case empty.
