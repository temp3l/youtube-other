## Narration Script

Every photograph of the flood showed the same man. He was always dry beneath the rain—and always
facing Laura's house. Laura investigates reports of a silent man appearing before flash floods.
He appears outside homes where someone will disappear before morning. The man leaves wet
footprints that begin at the door and end beneath the floorboards. The storm stops, but the rain
man enters the television studio through a live weather screen. Afterward, Laura is gone and
every forecast shows a small figure standing beneath the rain icon.
