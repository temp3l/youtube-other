## Narration Script

Tom never saw the tall man with his own eyes. He only appeared in photographs—and each time he
was closer. Tom photographs a missing-person search near the forest. Older newspaper archives
show the same figure at disappearances dating back decades. Tom begins losing facial details in
photographs of himself. He publishes every photograph at once, believing widespread attention
will weaken the entity. The next morning, readers report that faces are missing from all the
photographs except the tall man's.
