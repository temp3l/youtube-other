# Narration Script

Nina lived on the top floor, yet every night someone moved furniture above her ceiling. The warning
was already present: The landlord said never to open the maintenance door after midnight. A
staircase appeared behind it and led above the roof. The upper flat was fully furnished and
contained photographs of Nina at different ages. The behaviour followed one rule: The flat appeared
for tenants preparing to leave and arranged their lives one day ahead. A neighbour claimed every
former tenant who slept there vanished from the lower building. The situation became personal after
that. Nina found copies of her belongings positioned for events that had not happened. Then the
recurring sound returned from a place it could not physically reach. The only workable plan was
this: She refused to sleep there and destroyed the hidden staircase. The upper flat began descending
through the ceiling, replacing her real apartment room by room. Nina escaped by removing every
duplicated object before sunrise. For several seconds, the outcome remained uncertain. The noises
stopped. Weeks later, rain began leaking upward from Nina's ceiling.
