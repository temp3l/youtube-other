# Narration Script

The taxi unlocked itself when Daniel approached. There was no driver, but the navigation screen
already displayed his childhood address. The warning was already present: The booking app warned him
not to select a destination from memory. The doors locked as soon as he sat down. The taxi stopped
at places connected to people Daniel had lost. The behaviour followed one rule: The car collected
passengers who refused to accept a final goodbye. The radio asked Daniel to choose one person to
leave behind. The situation became personal after that. Each stop replayed a memory more accurately
than Daniel remembered it. Then the recurring sound returned from a place it could not physically
reach. The only workable plan was this: He refused every destination and used the emergency release
when the car slowed. At a red light, the taxi offered one final stop where all the dead were
waiting. Daniel pulled the release and jumped before the light changed. The taxi vanished before
police arrived. Daniel received a receipt listing his destination as 'Return Pending.'
