# Narration Script

The lift had three basement levels. During the blackout test, a new button appeared beneath them,
marked simply zero. The doors opened onto an unfinished office level with every desk lamp switched
on. Employee files belonged to people who had vanished from the tower. The behaviour followed one
rule: Basement Zero stored everything the building wanted forgotten and demanded one memory from
anyone who entered. Every desk held an object someone upstairs had reported missing. The situation
became personal after that. The intercom announced Nina's arrival before she left the lift. Then the
recurring sound returned from a place it could not physically reach. The only workable plan was
this: She inserted a false memory into the archive and offered it in place of a real one. The floor
began rearranging offices around her while the lift refused to return. Nina followed the one file
whose details changed each time she read it. The doors opened on the lobby and the zero button
vanished. Nina could no longer remember why elevators terrified her.
