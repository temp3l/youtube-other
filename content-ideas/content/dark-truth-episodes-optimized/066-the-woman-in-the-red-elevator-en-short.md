already present: A maintenance log said not to follow where she pointed unless the lift moved in the
wrong direction. She never pressed a button. The elevator descended while the floor display counted

reports. Then the recurring sound returned from a place it could not physically reach. The only
workable plan was this: Elena stopped the elevator and evacuated the building. The lift tried to
continue while alarms remained silent. Elena manually opened the service panel and triggered a
building-wide fault before the main cable snapped. For several seconds, the outcome remained
uncertain. No one was injured. Every elevator Elena later entered briefly displayed Floor Fourteen.
