different. The song used words Emma's mother had sung in childhood. The forest became silent
whenever anyone followed the voice. The behaviour followed one rule: The siren could imitate

phrase correctly. They rescued one surviving hiker. Back home, Emma heard the private phrase sung
from the trees outside her window.
