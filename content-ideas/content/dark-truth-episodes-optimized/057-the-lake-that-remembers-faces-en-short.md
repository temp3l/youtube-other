# Narration Script

Faces appeared beneath the perfectly still water at night. Local families recognised people who had
vanished years earlier. The reflections moved even when the surface was disturbed. Adrian saw his
own face among them before entering the water. The behaviour followed one rule: The lake preserved
the final memory of everyone who died nearby and used those memories to guide new victims. A camera
recovered underwater contained footage of Adrian's future dive. The situation became personal after
that. The footage showed him following an exact route toward a collapsed church. Then the recurring
sound returned from a place it could not physically reach. The only workable plan was this: Adrian
deliberately chose a different route and tracked the underwater knocks instead. The faces tried to
redirect him while the future footage updated inside the camera housing. Adrian ignored his own
reflection and found a living diver trapped beneath wreckage. He rescued the diver. Afterward, every
mirror Adrian owned showed rippling water behind him.
