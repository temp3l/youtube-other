## Reusable recommendations

1. Start with the impossible detail before introducing background.
2. Give each threat one stable, testable rule.
3. Tie the sound motif only to escalation points.
4. Include a reasonable failed safety response.
5. Use investigation and evidence to add meaningful runtime.
6. Let the protagonist solve the climax through observation rather than force.
7. Follow the apparent escape with one concise final contradiction.
8. Preserve short dialogue and clean sentence rhythm for TTS.
