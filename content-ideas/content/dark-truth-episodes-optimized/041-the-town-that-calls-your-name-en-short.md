# Narration Script

The first voice called Adrian's name from an empty house. The second came from behind him. The road
disappeared from his map as soon as the sun went down. A public loudspeaker announced private
memories only Adrian knew. The pattern followed one rule: Whenever Adrian spoke his own name,
streets rearranged and public records outside the town forgot another detail about him. A registry
listed previous visitors whose names had been erased line by line. His phone contacts lost his
surname, then his photographs, then every message he had sent. Adrian responded with another
practical measure: stopping the work, separating people from the source and calling for help. At
this point, the account accelerated. The only workable plan was this: Adrian recorded a false name
and played it through the town loudspeaker. Every house began calling the false name while he ran
toward the boundary. Then his dead father's voice called his real name from behind. The road
returned and Adrian's records slowly reappeared. Weeks later, strangers in his city began turning
toward him before anyone spoke.
