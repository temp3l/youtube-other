# Narration Script

At the final stop, every passenger left except one man seated at the back. The stop bell rang from
the empty seat beside him. The route display began naming cemeteries instead of streets. The
passenger asked whether Laura remembered the crash. The behaviour followed one rule: The bus
repeated the route until an unidentified victim was named. An old newspaper described a fatal
accident on the same route and one passenger never formally identified. The situation became
personal after that. The bus doors stopped responding and every road returned to the abandoned
terminal. Then the recurring sound returned from a place it could not physically reach. The only
workable plan was this: Laura drove to the original final stop and read the missing victim's name
aloud. The passenger stood for the first time, but the aisle stretched longer with every step. Laura
kept every door open and repeated the name until the lights returned. The passenger vanished. The
next night, someone boarded wearing Laura's uniform and sat in the last row.
