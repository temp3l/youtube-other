# Narration Script

The distress signal came from a ship that had been missing for thirty years. When Elena's crew found
it, the engines were still running and every light was on. The boarding team found meals set on
tables, warm machinery and no passengers. The behaviour followed one rule: The ship repeated its
final voyage until it collected a complete replacement crew. The situation became personal after
that. One crew member vanished from a sealed corridor and immediately appeared on the missing
vessel's manifest. Then the recurring sound returned from a place it could not physically reach. The
only workable plan was this: Elena flooded the navigation system with contradictory routes so the
ship could not complete a single final voyage. The ghost ship began turning in several directions at
once while footsteps moved through every deck. Elena crossed back to the research vessel as the
abandoned ship tried to match their heading. The vessel vanished before dawn. Elena's crew returned
safely, but every official passenger manifest now listed them aboard the missing ship.
