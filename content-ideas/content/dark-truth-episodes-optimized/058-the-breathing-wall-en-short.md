# Narration Script

The bedroom wall expanded and contracted like lungs. At night, the breathing matched Mia's own. The
warning was already present: A former tenant had written behind the radiator: do not cut the wall
while it is inhaling. A thermal camera revealed a human-shaped warm space inside the plaster.
Neighbours said earlier tenants had become unable to breathe outside the room. The behaviour
followed one rule: The wall learned each tenant's breathing pattern and made them dependent on it.
Building plans showed the apartment had been constructed around an older sealed house. The situation
became personal after that. When Mia cut a small opening, multiple voices whispered from inside.
Then the recurring sound returned from a place it could not physically reach. The only workable plan
was this: She interrupted the rhythm with fans, alarms and irregular bursts of air before sealing
the opening. The wall inhaled hard enough to drag furniture across the room. Mia escaped while the
entire apartment contracted around the old hidden structure. The building was evacuated. Demolition
footage showed the interior taking one final breath.
