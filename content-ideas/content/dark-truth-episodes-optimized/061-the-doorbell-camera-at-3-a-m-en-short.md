# Narration Script

At 3:00 a.m., Lukas received a motion alert showing himself standing outside while he was still
inside the house. The outside version stared into the lens without blinking. It began repeating
Lukas's private thoughts with a delay. The behaviour followed one rule: The camera uploaded footage
from several hours in the future, and each viewing made that future more likely. Older clips showed
the figure moving closer to the door every night. The situation became personal after that.
Disabling power did not stop the doorbell from ringing. Then the recurring sound returned from a
place it could not physically reach. The only workable plan was this: Lukas played a recorded false
routine to lure the future version toward the garage. The porch figure followed the fake sounds
while footage inside the house showed another person opening the front door. Lukas had to choose
which prediction to break. Police found the porch empty. The final clip showed Lukas asleep while
someone already inside opened the door.
