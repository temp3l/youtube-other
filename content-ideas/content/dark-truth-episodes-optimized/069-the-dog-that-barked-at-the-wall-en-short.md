# Narration Script

Every night, Paul's dog barked at the same blank section of wall and refused to enter the room. The
warning was already present: The renovation plans contained a handwritten note: second room not
opened. A thermal camera showed a cold human-shaped space inside the wall. Scratching began whenever
Paul turned off the lights. The behaviour followed one rule: The hidden room allowed someone inside
to watch the house through narrow openings. Building plans showed a sealed room omitted from the
renovation. The situation became personal after that. Inside, Paul found a chair facing outward and
photographs of previous families. Then the recurring sound returned from a place it could not
physically reach. The only workable plan was this: He opened the wall while keeping the dog close
and called police before entering. A figure rose from the chair when Paul stepped inside. The dog
pulled him backward as the room's only door sealed itself. For several seconds, the outcome remained
uncertain. Police found the hidden room empty. That night, the dog began barking at the wall behind
Paul's bed.
