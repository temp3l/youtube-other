# Narration Script

Clara saw a child writing tomorrow's date on the blackboard inside a classroom locked for years. The
warning was already present: A faded sign on the door said attendance must never be taken after
midnight. The room was empty when Clara entered, but the chalk was still moving. The same child
appeared in class photographs from different decades. The behaviour followed one rule: Each night,
the board listed people the school intended to erase by morning. A sealed register contained
children officially never enrolled. The situation became personal after that. One teacher whose name
appeared on the board vanished before sunrise. Then the recurring sound returned from a place it
could not physically reach. The only workable plan was this: Clara read every forgotten child's name
aloud and completed the attendance register. The bell rang repeatedly while desks filled with unseen
pupils. Clara continued until the final name answered from directly behind her. For several seconds,
the outcome remained uncertain. The classroom door opened. The next day, an empty desk appeared in
Clara's office with her name on it.
