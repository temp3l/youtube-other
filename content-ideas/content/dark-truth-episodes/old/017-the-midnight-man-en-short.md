# Short 017 — They Invited the Midnight Man Inside

## Narration Instructions

- Use the same narrator as the full episode.
- Keep the pace close to 180 words per minute.
- Begin immediately and keep the hook visible from the first sentence.

## Narration Script

The ritual required Daniel to carry a candle until 3:33 a.m. The first time it went out, something whispered from inside his shadow.

Daniel Cross was a sceptical livestream host when the story began in an abandoned country house used for an online challenge. The first reports did not describe an obvious monster or violent attack. They described a candle flame turning blue when the entity is near. That detail mattered because it returned at moments when no ordinary source could be found.

Most frightening stories become exaggerated after they are repeated. This one became more precise. Dates, rooms, recordings and witness statements differed in minor ways, but the central sequence remained the same. A warning was ignored, an impossible pattern appeared, and the person at the centre of it survived with evidence that made survival difficult to celebrate.

The first event seemed harmless enough to rationalise. Daniel and two friends perform the ritual for a livestream audience. The setting was described in enough detail to make the account testable: the time, weather and nearby objects were all recorded. Later, those ordinary details became important because several of them changed without explanation.
