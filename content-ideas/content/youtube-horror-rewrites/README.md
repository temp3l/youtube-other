# Professional YouTube Horror Rewrites

This package contains four rewritten horror stories in:
- English long-form
- English YouTube Short
- German long-form
- German YouTube Short

The scripts are written for spoken narration, with stronger hooks, concrete scenes, escalating stakes, clear supernatural rules, and final stingers.

## Editorial note

- Kisaragi Station, the Bell Witch and the Russian Sleep Experiment are based on existing legends or internet folklore.
- Present them as adaptations, retellings or fictionalised stories rather than verified documentary accounts.
- Long scripts are compact production drafts and may be expanded further for 10–12 minute videos through atmospheric scene detail and pauses.
