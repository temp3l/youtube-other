# Copyright Risk Analysis

## 1. Fictional Stories

### Pros

- Very low copyright risk if entirely AI-generated.
- You own the script.
- Can generate unlimited content.
- Easy to localize into multiple languages.

### Cons

- Difficult to build trust initially.
- Requires strong storytelling.
- Generic stories often perform poorly.

### Copyright Risk

- Low if fully original.
- High if inspired too closely by:

  - Harry Potter
  - Star Wars
  - Marvel
  - Existing creepypastas

### Recommendation

★★★★★ Excellent for automation.

---

## 2. Urban Legends

### Pros

- Strong retention.
- Great for shorts.
- Huge audience interest.

### Cons

- Facts are often unverifiable.
- Risk of repeating copyrighted modern retellings.

### Copyright Risk

- Low when using original narration.
- Medium when copying:

  - Reddit posts
  - Blog articles
  - YouTube scripts

### Recommendation

★★★★☆ Good.

# Best Niches for Fully Automated AI Channels

### Tier 1 (Lowest Copyright Risk + High Revenue)

1. Programming lessons
2. Financial strategies
3. Historical facts
4. Science explanations
5. Psychology and human behavior
6. Space and astronomy

**Copyright risk:** ★☆☆☆☆ (Very Low)

---

### Tier 2 (Excellent for Viral Growth)

1. Urban legends
2. Mythology and folklore
3. Crime and mystery
4. Philosophy

**Copyright risk:** ★★☆☆☆ (Low)

---

### Tier 3 (Highest Growth Potential but More Risk)

1. AI news
2. Celebrity news
3. Movie explanations
4. TV-show recaps
5. Sports highlights

**Copyright risk:** ★★★★☆ to ★★★★★

These often trigger copyright claims because they rely on third-party footage, images, broadcasts, or scripts.

For a fully automated pipeline using AI-generated scripts, AI voice, AI images, and AI video, the safest combination is:

- Historical facts
- Psychology
- Science
- Space
- Programming
- Finance
- Mythology

Those niches can realistically scale to hundreds or thousands of videos with minimal copyright exposure if you generate the narration, visuals, and edits yourself rather than reusing existing media.
